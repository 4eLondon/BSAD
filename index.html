About RoadReady JA

Removing Roadblocks,
One Click at a Time

RoadReady JA was founded on 23 February 2026 by Management Information Systems students who saw a clear need: Jamaica's public services were moving online, but the licence process hadn't kept up.

We built a platform that removes the uncertainty and inconvenience of in-person visits — no more arriving at TAJ only to be told your licence isn't ready. Our system connects citizens with the services they need, securely and without delay.

"To provide digital technology that simplifies and improves everyday business processes."
